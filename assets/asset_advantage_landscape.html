Quadrant Key
The Directory
Static data. No signal or interpretation.
The Feed
Live signals. No finished intelligence layer.
The Analyst
Action-ready insight. Human-paced, unscalable.
The Engine (border)
Account-level intelligence. Contact gap remains.
The Engine
Live signals plus contact-level, action-ready output.
The critical distinction
For most vendors in the Engine quadrant, the intelligence stops at the account level. A Managed Intelligence Partner operates at the contact level, delivering who to call, when, and what to say.
The two failure modes
Freshness Problem. Most data is stale before a rep touches it. Moving right means signals that reflect reality today.
Relevance Problem. Even fresh signals don't direct action. Moving up means the intelligence layer, not the rep, has translated data into context.
