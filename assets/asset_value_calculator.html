How the model works. Hours are the unit; the blended rate converts dollars to hours. Total growth time is set at 10% of your annual booked target, the firm-wide effort that goes into winning new work, weighted toward growth leaders but carried in volume by junior staff. That time splits between research, the targeting and qualification the engine can run, and engagement, the networking and active-opportunity work that stays with your people. The first slider sets that split. The engine reclaims a share of the research half, set by the second slider. Reclaimed hours are then valued at your blended billable rate, assuming they redeploy to billable work. All figures illustrative, computed on your inputs. Not a guarantee of results.
